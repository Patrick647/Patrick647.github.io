## Overview

**Duration**: January 2024 – November 2024

**Role**: Team Member & Presenter

**Team**: "Yule Circle" (渔乐圈)

## Project Focus

Addressing medication safety challenges for breastfeeding women in Wuxi region through a cross-departmental approach involving pharmacy, maternal health, community hospitals, and specialist nurses.

## Methodology

Applied PDCA methodology to develop a "maternal-child model" for pharmaceutical services, leveraging:
- National Baby-Friendly Hospital resources
- Provincial Mother-Baby Friendly Hospital platform
- Jiangsu Pharmaceutical Association Science Popularization Base
- Wuxi Maternal and Child Health Network
