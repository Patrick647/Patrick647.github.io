## Overview

**Duration**: May 2023 – August 2023

**Institution**: King's College London

**Degree**: Master's Thesis

## Research Objective

Develop a high-sensitivity aptasensor for early diagnosis of breast cancer through detection of EpCAM proteins and exosomes.

## Methodology

Systematically optimized critical parameters including:
- Volume of Carboxylic MXene
- Concentration of the Aptamer
- Duration of Aptamer incubation and reaction

## Results

The aptasensor demonstrated:
- Large detection range
- Low limit of detection
- Enhanced sensitivity for EpCAM protein and exosome detection
