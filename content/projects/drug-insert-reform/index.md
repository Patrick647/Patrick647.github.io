## Overview

**Role**: Core Team Member
**Period**: January 2020 – April 2025

A comprehensive initiative to transform drug package inserts into patient-friendly visual formats.

## Key Achievements

- **150+ visualized medication instruction videos** covering 5 therapeutic categories
- **850,000+ patients** benefited with **100% satisfaction rate**
- **"One Drug One QR Code" system** - independently designed and implemented
- Led the **"Anxin Pharmacist" team** of 14 specialized pharmacists (70%+ with master's/doctoral degrees)

## Innovation

Identified limitations of national reform approaches (enlarged fonts, electronic versions) and proposed patient-centered visualization solutions addressing comprehension barriers.

- First Batch of Jiangsu Pharmaceutical Association "Pharmacy Science Popularization Base" (April 2024)
